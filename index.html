How it works

Two sports. One score.

Any walking pad, any game, any pace. Get moving and get on the board.

1
Set up your pad
Get on your walking pad or treadmill. Connect your Strava account so your speed and distance are recorded automatically.
2
Start running and playing
Launch your speedrun and start walking or jogging. Record your screen so both the game and treadmill console are visible.
3
Submit your run
Upload your video link, enter your game time and average MPH. A moderator verifies it before it goes live on the leaderboard.
4
Climb the leaderboard
Your score combines game time and running speed. Run faster or play faster — both improve your rank.
The formula

Simple, fair, and adjustable

Every game category can set its own weights — but the default is equal: game time and running speed matter equally.

Score = Game time (min) ÷ Avg MPH
Lower score = better. Finish faster AND run faster to win.

Features

Everything you need to compete

Dual progress tracking
Watch your speedrun PBs and walking pace improve side by side over time. The only platform that tracks both.
Live leaderboards
Separate boards per game and category, with adjustable speed and time weights set by the community.
Strava sync
Connect Strava once. Every treadmill session auto-imports your speed, distance, and heart rate for verification.
Verified runs
Every run is reviewed by community moderators before going live. Video proof + Strava data required.
speedrun.com linked
Your speedrun.com PBs sync automatically. No double entry — your gaming history is already here.
Community rules
Game categories are community-governed, just like speedrun.com. Runners set the rules, moderators enforce them.
